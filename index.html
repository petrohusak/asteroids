Welcome to the game "Asteroid".

Some rules:

  • At this game you have a starship, that must destroy asteroids as much, as possible.
  • You are flying in the direction of the cursor.
  • You have infinity bullets.
  • You shoot in the direction you are flying.
  • For each large destroyed asteroid you get 100 points, for each small - 150.
  • Small asteroids are slightly some faster, than large ones.
